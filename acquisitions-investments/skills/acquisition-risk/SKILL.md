# Acquisition Risk

Comprehensive risk taxonomy and due diligence checklist framework for CRE acquisitions. Used by `/diligence-punchlist` and `/screen-deal` for risk identification.

## Risk Taxonomy

### Market Risk
- Submarket oversupply (construction pipeline > absorption)
- Rent decline trajectory
- Major employer departure or downsizing
- Demographic shifts (population, income)
- Regulatory changes (rent control, zoning)

### Tenant Risk
- Single-tenant concentration (>25% of rent)
- Near-term expirations without renewal visibility
- Tenant credit deterioration
- Below-market leases rolling to market (downside if market has fallen)
- Co-tenancy or kickout clauses (retail)

### Physical Risk
- Deferred maintenance / capital backlog
- Structural issues (foundation, roof, HVAC at end-of-life)
- Code compliance gaps
- ADA compliance
- Seismic (West Coast), hurricane (Southeast/Gulf), flood zone

### Environmental Risk
- Phase I findings requiring Phase II
- Known contamination (soil, groundwater)
- Asbestos, lead paint, mold
- Underground storage tanks
- Adjacent property contamination risk

### Financial Risk
- Negative leverage (cap rate < borrowing cost)
- Thin DSCR coverage
- Floating rate exposure without cap
- Refi risk at maturity
- Tax assessment appeal risk

### Legal / Title Risk
- Title defects or encumbrances
- Ongoing litigation
- Lease enforceability issues
- Easements restricting use
- HOA or CC&R restrictions

## Due Diligence Checklist by Property Type

### All Types (Standard)
- [ ] Title commitment and survey
- [ ] Phase I environmental site assessment
- [ ] Property condition assessment (PCA)
- [ ] Rent roll and lease abstracts
- [ ] Trailing financial statements (3 years + T-12)
- [ ] Tax returns or assessments
- [ ] Insurance loss history
- [ ] Zoning confirmation letter
- [ ] Estoppel certificates from tenants
- [ ] Service contracts review

### Office (Additional)
- [ ] Building systems assessment (HVAC, elevator, BMS)
- [ ] Parking ratio adequacy
- [ ] TI obligation analysis for upcoming rollovers
- [ ] Amenity competitiveness assessment

### Industrial (Additional)
- [ ] Clear height and loading capacity verification
- [ ] Truck court and trailer parking adequacy
- [ ] Power capacity (amps, phase)
- [ ] Rail access (if applicable)
- [ ] Environmental — chemical storage, waste handling

### Multifamily (Additional)
- [ ] Unit condition audit (sample inspection)
- [ ] Rent control / stabilization compliance
- [ ] Utility system (master vs. individual metering)
- [ ] Amenity package competitiveness
- [ ] Crime / safety assessment

### Retail (Additional)
- [ ] Tenant sales reports (percentage rent basis)
- [ ] Co-tenancy and exclusive use clauses
- [ ] Trade area demographics
- [ ] Anchor tenant viability
- [ ] Pad site and outparcel ground leases
