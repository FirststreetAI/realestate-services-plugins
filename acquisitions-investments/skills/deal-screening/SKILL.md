# Deal Screening

Framework for quickly evaluating whether an acquisition opportunity warrants further pursuit based on fund strategy, size, market, and return thresholds.

## Screening Criteria by Strategy

### Core
- Stabilized, well-leased (>90% occupancy)
- Primary markets
- Institutional quality / recent vintage
- Going-in yield: 4.5-6.0%
- Target IRR: 7-9%
- Max leverage: 50% LTV

### Core-Plus
- Stabilized with modest value-add opportunity
- Primary and strong secondary markets
- Some lease-up or minor capex opportunity
- Going-in yield: 5.0-7.0%
- Target IRR: 9-12%
- Max leverage: 60% LTV

### Value-Add
- Below-market occupancy or rents
- Significant capital or repositioning needed
- Primary through tertiary markets (market-dependent)
- Going-in yield: 5.5-8.0%
- Target IRR: 12-18%
- Max leverage: 65-70% LTV

### Opportunistic
- Development, major repositioning, distress
- Any market with thesis support
- Going-in yield: variable (may be negative initially)
- Target IRR: 18%+
- Max leverage: 70-80% LTV

## Go / No-Go Decision Framework

**Automatic GO**: Meets all fund criteria, no red flags
**Conditional GO**: Meets most criteria, 1-2 manageable exceptions
**NO-GO flags**:
- Property type outside mandate
- Market outside target geography
- Size outside fund range (too small or too large)
- Seller ask implies return below fund minimum
- Unremediated environmental contamination
- Major structural deficiency
- Litigation encumbering title
